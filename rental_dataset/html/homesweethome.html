Area

The Gozsdu Court is a speciality in Budapest's downtown. It is located just a couple of minutes walk from Deák Square, the busyest interchange in Budapest. The renovated Gozsdu Court will have the ambience of Váci utca spiced with the bustle of Liszt Ferenc quare, and will become not simply an unrivalled architectural monument but also a cultural venue for the city centre, offering a constantly-chaining programme of events. This area continues to be the centre of life in terms of culture and business, it is rich in historical monuments and especially rich in architectural treasures. The picturesque Andrássy Avenue, trendy Király street, always busy Liszt Ferenc Square, the Music Academy and the "Pesti Broadway" with the Opera House and theaters are all featuring the environment. The St.Stephens Basilica, great Synagoge can be found in a distance of just a few minutes' walk. This area having excellent transport connections and a unique atmosphere is one of such quarters, which make Budapest one of the world's most wonderful capitals.


Transportation

Deák square, the main public transportation spot of Budapest is just a minute walk.  All 3 metro lines meet here, together with several bus and tram lines. The Ringroad with 4-6 trams as an easy walk as well. You can be anywhere in the city quickly.
